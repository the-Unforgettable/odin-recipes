Chicken Teryaki

Savor the irresistible flavor of our Chicken Teriyaki sandwich, featuring tender, marinated chicken strips glazed in a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce. This sandwich comes on your choice of freshly baked bread, layered with crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and a dash of onions for a refreshing crunch. Customizable Toppings: Add your favorite veggies like cucumbers, green peppers, or spinach. Top it off with sauces such as sweet onion, mayo, or chipotle southwest for a personalized twist.

Ingredients:

  • Freshly baked bread (choice of type)
  • Grilled chicken strips
  • Teriyaki sauce
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Optional: cucumbers, green peppers, spinach, sauces

Steps:

  1. Start by selecting your preferred type of bread and slice it open.
  2. Warm the grilled chicken strips and toss them in teriyaki sauce.
  3. Layer the chicken on the bread as the base.
  4. Add fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and onions on top.
  5. Customize with additional veggies and sauces as desired.
  6. Toast the sandwich lightly if preferred.
  7. Serve and enjoy your Chicken Teriyaki sandwich!
