Italien B.M.T

Dive into the bold flavors of our Italian B.M.T., stacked with layers of pepperoni, salami, and ham on freshly baked bread. It’s topped with crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and onions for a perfect balance of zest and freshness. Customizable Toppings: Add some banana peppers, olives, or spinach to elevate the flavors. Drizzle with Italian dressing, honey mustard, or a splash of hot sauce for an extra zing. Each sub is crafted to perfection and fully customizable—because at Subway, your sandwich is your canvas!

Ingredients:

  • Freshly baked bread (choice of type)
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Salami slices
  • Ham slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Optional: banana peppers, olives, spinach, sauces

Steps:

  1. Choose your favorite bread and slice it open.
  2. Layer slices of pepperoni, salami, and ham as the base.
  3. Add fresh lettuce, tomatoes, and onions on top.
  4. Include optional toppings like banana peppers or olives for added flavor.
  5. Drizzle with your preferred sauce such as Italian dressing or honey mustard.
  6. Toast lightly if preferred.
  7. Serve and enjoy your Italian B.M.T. sandwich!
